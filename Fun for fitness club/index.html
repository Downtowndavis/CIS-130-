Fun for Fitness Club

Where fun and fitness meet!

What makes us different?

Here at Fun for Fitness Club we pride ourselves on helping you accomplish your health and fitness goals, while also creating a fun and non-judgemental enviroment. Staffed by certified personal trainers, each location is able to help you meet any goal you may be pushing to achieve. Our gyms are open 24/7, which allows you to plan your fitness around your time and not when the gym is open. Strength training? We have free-weights and resitance machines for your use! Looking to lose weight? Our cardio section has all the machines you want, from treadmills, to stair climbers.

Why you should join!

Our memberships start at $10 a month for the basic package, cheaper than any gym in town! We offer several classes such as, Zumba, BodyPump, BodyCombat, and several others. These classes are free to members and if you want to try them out, you can click the "More Information" tab at the top of the page to request a free trial of any of our classes.
